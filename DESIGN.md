# ProofBook design language

Warm editorial utility: paper canvas, dark green ink, restrained terracotta accent, sharp geometry, generous whitespace, and one obvious action. No gradients, glass cards, emoji, or generic dashboard chrome. The confirmation is the visual proof moment.
